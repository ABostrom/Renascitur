In his moment of triumph, [[Vecna]] is betrayed by **Kas**, now corrupted by the Blade of [[Typhon, The Archon of Death|Typhon]]. [[Vecna]] is slain, and Kas becomes the **first vampire**, ending the Age in shadow.
